Our History
Professional Development Opportunities: Professional development opportunities refer to activities and resources that enhance the knowledge, skills, and competencies of professionals in their respective fields. The Kenya National Association of Social Workers (KNASW) recognizes the importance of continuous professional development for social workers and offers various opportunities for members to further their knowledge and expertise. These opportunities may include workshops, seminars, webinars, training programs, and academic courses. By engaging in professional development, social workers can stay updated with the latest research, best practices, and emerging trends in the field, ultimately enhancing their effectiveness in supporting individuals, families, and communities.

Conferences and Events: Conferences and events organized by the KNASW provide platforms for social workers to connect, exchange ideas, and learn from experts and peers in the field. These gatherings offer opportunities to attend keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and networking sessions. Conferences and events bring together professionals from various sectors, allowing social workers to gain insights into interdisciplinary approaches, collaborate with other professionals, and expand their professional networks. The KNASW may organize annual conferences, regional events, or thematic symposiums to address specific issues and promote professional growth and collaboration among its members.

Certification and Licensure: Certification and licensure serve as markers of professional competence and credibility in the social work profession. The KNASW may collaborate with relevant regulatory bodies to establish certification or licensure programs that validate the qualifications and expertise of social workers. Certification typically involves meeting specific education, experience, and examination requirements, while licensure may also require adherence to ethical standards and demonstration of clinical competence. These certifications and licenses provide recognition and assurance to employers, clients, and the public that social workers meet certain standards of professional practice.

By providing professional development opportunities, organizing conferences and events, and endorsing certification and licensure programs, the KNASW supports the ongoing growth and advancement of social workers. These initiatives enhance the professional knowledge and skills of members, promote collaboration and networking, and ensure that social workers maintain the highest standards of practice. Ultimately, these efforts contribute to the delivery of quality services and the overall development of the social work profession in Kenya.
